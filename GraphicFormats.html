JP(E)G:

Kind of graphical content: photo
Compression: supported, lossy
Motion picture support: no

GIF:

Kind of graphical content: graphic
Compression: supported, lossless
Motion picture support: limited to short, low-resolution videos

PNG:

Kind of graphical content: graphic, photo
Compression: supported, lossless
Motion picture support: no

ICO:

Kind of graphical content: icon
Compression: supported, lossless
Motion picture support: no

SVG:

Kind of graphical content: graphic, scalable vector graphic
Compression: supported, lossless
Motion picture support: no

MPEG / MP4:

Kind of graphical content: video
Compression: supported, lossy
Motion picture support: yes

In general, JPEG is a good choice for photographs because it supports lossy compression, which allows for smaller file sizes without significantly degrading the quality of the image.
GIF is a good choice for graphics with a limited number of colors, such as logos or icons, because it supports lossless compression.
PNG is a good choice for graphics that require transparency, such as logos with a transparent background.
ICO is a good choice for icons because it is a specialized format for icons. SVG is a good choice for graphics that need to be scaled, such as logos, because it is a scalable vector graphic format.
MPEG and MP4 are good choices for motion pictures because they support video.
